11 Nights · 13–24 July 2026

The Itinerary

An active-but-relaxed plan: beach and pool downtime balanced with surf, climbing, horse riding, a Berlengas boat trip, and day trips around the Silver Coast.

! Book & prep before you go

Berlengas boat + BerlengasPass.

Strict daily visitor cap. Book the boat (Viamar / BerlengaTur from Peniche, ~€25–30 return/adult) and the separate ICNF permit at berlengaspass.icnf.pt (~€7 adult / €3.50 child) 2–3 weeks ahead for July.

Surf lessons & horse riding.

Reserve a Baleal surf school and Eco Salgados (horse riding, in Atouguia da Baleia) a few days ahead.

Tourist tax — cash on arrival.

€1/person/night, capped at €5/person, under-13s exempt. For our group ≈ €20 in cash.

Accommodation bulletin.

Complete the passport-details form the host sends before check-in (legal requirement).

☼ Day by day

Day 1 · Sun 13 July

Arrival

Land ~15:45, collect the rental, drive ~1h10 to the villa (check-in from 16:00). Stock up at a supermarket near Peniche. Settle in, swim, easy first dinner — grilled fish at Prainha Wine Bar or Tasca do Joel in Baleal.

Day 2 · Mon 14 July

Ease in at Baleal

Beach and pool day. Baleal's sheltered side is shallow and calm — good for everyone. Walk out to Baleal island at low tide.

Day 3 · Tue 15 July

Surf morning

Group beginner lesson at Baleal Norte or Prainha (the gentlest wave around). Afternoon pool recovery.

Day 4 · Wed 16 July

Berlengas boat trip (booked)

Full day. 30-min crossing, snorkelling in clear coves, the São João Baptista fort, cave tour if offered. Pick a calm-forecast day. Relaxed dinner at the villa.

Day 5 · Thu 17 July

Peniche town + Cabo Carvoeiro

Fortress museum, harbour lunch, then Cabo Carvoeiro — the cliffs, Nau dos Corvos and Remédios chapel — timed for sunset.

Day 6 · Fri 18 July

Óbidos + Nazaré day trip

Óbidos: walk the medieval walls, Rua Direita, ginjinha. Then Nazaré: funicular to Sítio, the viewpoints over the big-wave beach, and a swim at the town beach. Full Nazaré guide →

Day 7 · Sat 19 July

Downtime

Deliberately unplanned. Pool, books, a long lunch, sunset stroll on the Papôa promontory.

Day 8 · Sun 20 July

Horse riding on the beach

Morning ride with Eco Salgados in Atouguia da Baleia — gentle beginner/kids rides along the coast. Afternoon at the pool or Gigi beach.

Day 9 · Mon 21 July

Climbing + second surf

Morning at The West Climbing Center in Peniche (bouldering, auto-belays, moonboard). Optional afternoon surf session.

Day 10 · Tue 22 July

Coast explore or extra day trip

Either beach-hop the Peniche coves with a seafood lunch at Toca do Texugo, or a half-day north to the Alcobaça & Batalha monasteries.

Day 11 · Wed 23 July

Last full day

Favourite beach, pack in the afternoon, special final dinner in Baleal for sunset.

Day 12 · Thu 24 July

Departure

Checkout by 11:00. Drive back to Lisbon airport (~1h15, plus rental drop-off buffer).

Wind days are flexible. On a good forecast (6+ m/s), swap a beach day for a wing session — see the wing-foil spot map. Keep the Berlengas day flexible against the marine forecast, since it's the one activity the weather can cancel.
