Wing Foiling the Silver Coast

Ocean wave-riding spots keyed to the summer nortada, plus flat-water and freeride options. Base: Atouguia da Baleia.

The peninsula rule. Peniche juts due west, so it almost always offers a leeward, cross/side-shore option — pick the south side in nortada, the north side in SW/frontal wind. That flexibility, minutes from the villa, is the region's biggest asset.
